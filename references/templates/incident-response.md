# Incident Response

## Trigger

Describe the release, build, service, save, economy, or community issue.

## Severity

Classify player impact, platform impact, data risk, and reputation risk.

## Response

Name owner, communication channel, fix path, validation path, and rollback path.

## Player communication

Draft short status, known issue, and resolution notes.

## Retrospective

Capture cause, detection gap, prevention, and follow-up verification.
